# GIS Portfolio

### Ethiopia Drought Spatial Analysis 2021 - 2022 
* Conducted an extensive research report on droughts in Ethiopia and analysed large datasets from various sources to draw conclusions on spatial relationships of water points within different regions in the country
* Identified clustering patterns of water points using spatial autocorrelation and descriptive statistics 
* Nominated for posting on ESRI ECCE Research Blog, an undergrad academic community of all things GIS

### COVID-19 and Crime Spatial Analysis 
* Conducted research on the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic on crime rates in Toronto presented using ArcGIS Pro
* Designed proportional symbology maps depicting positive correlations between Toronto crime rates and COVID-19

### COVID-19 and Homeless Shelters in Toronto 
* Collaborated within a team of three to research and report on spatial relationships between Toronto Homeless shelters and COVID-19 from 2018 to 2022
* Conducted a temporal spatial analysis to determine spatial clustering of temporary homeless shelters and COVID-19 outbreaks
